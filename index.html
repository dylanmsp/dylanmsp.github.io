TPM+™

TPM+™ provides a reliable encrypted backup service to the cloud. A heavy shift from on-site backups to cloud-based backups is now proven and accepted. Traditional on-site backup means someone has to remember to change tapes or swap media, bringing with it all the potential problems should data not be available to restore.

There are a large number of online backup solutions in the market and the concept of cloud backups is both proven and accepted. Our hybrid cloud-based backup and recovery solution integrates with our remote IT management software to provide you with a cohesive single-faced solution at a lower cost than most online backup vendors.

We use backup technology which searches for changes at disk block level. On average, only 0.1 - 0.5% of the protected data set there has any changes, making it highly effective at maximizing bandwidth and storage capacity. We also utilize technology that simultaneously backs data up locally as well as to the cloud, providing near-instant access to important files.
